Near-Earth Objects (NEOs)

Near-Earth Objects (NEOs) are asteroids and comets that orbit the Sun and come close to Earth's orbit. These celestial bodies are of particular interest to scientists because of their proximity and potential to impact Earth. NEOs include both asteroids, which are rocky bodies, and comets, which are icy.
